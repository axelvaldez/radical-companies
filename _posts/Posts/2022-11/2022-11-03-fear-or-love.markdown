<h1>&nbsp;</h1>
 <p>It is simple: Risk life or preserve it. Go after it or run away from it. Fear or love.</p>

<h1>Risk Life or Preserve It</h1>
 <p>Unicellular bacteria have this wired into them. &ldquo;Hmm&hellip; nourishment, go after it! Hmm&hellip; poison, run away from it!&rdquo;</p>
 <p>Risk or preserve life: the rule of all live creatures.</p>

<h1>Guided by Love</h1>
 <p>I prefer to be guided by love. The kind that leads with trust, and tries to touch the worldview that could support the words said. And, yes, if somebody attacks me, I'll defend my life.</p>
 <p>Being guided by love is good for my wellbeing and those around me (like you).</p>
